Common questions
Is this coaching or consulting? +

It’s coaching — which means I don’t tell you what to do. I use certified assessments, research-backed tools, and precise questions to help you think more clearly and act more decisively. You make the decisions. I help you make better ones.

I’m based outside the UAE. Can we work together? +

Yes. Sessions are available online globally. I work with leaders across Europe, Asia, and the Gulf. The EQ-i 2.0 assessment is delivered digitally and debriefed via video call.

How long does a programme typically take? +

The individual assessment and debrief takes one session. The 90-Day Leadership Edge runs over three months with bi-weekly sessions. Corporate programmes are scoped to your organisation’s needs and timeline.

What makes this different from other leadership coaching? +

Two things: it starts with data, not conversation. And it ends with measurement, not impressions. The EQ-i 2.0 is the world’s most validated emotional intelligence assessment. Every programme begins by measuring exactly where you are — and ends by measuring how far you’ve moved.
